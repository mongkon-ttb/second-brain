# Random Facts

## Science & Nature 🌿
- **Bananas are berries**, but strawberries are not! 🍌🍓
- **Water can boil and freeze at the same time** in a process called the triple point.
- **A day on Venus** is longer than a year on Venus. 😲

## Space & Astronomy 🚀
- **There’s a huge water cloud in space**—12 billion light-years away, containing 140 trillion times the water in Earth’s oceans. 💦
- **Neutron stars are incredibly dense**—a sugar-cube-sized piece would weigh about a billion tons!
- **Jupiter has a moon (Ganymede) larger than Mercury**. 🪐

## History & Culture 🏛️
- **Cleopatra lived closer to the invention of the iPhone than to the construction of the Great Pyramid.** 📱
- **Oxford University is older than the Aztec Empire.** 🎓
- **The Eiffel Tower can grow taller in summer** due to metal expansion. 🌞

## Technology & Innovations 💡
- **The first computer bug was a real insect**—a moth found in a Harvard Mark II computer in 1947. 🦋
- **NASA’s internet speed** can reach 91 gigabits per second. 🚀
- **There are more real-life Lego minifigures than people on Earth.** 🏗️

## Fun & Weird Facts 🎉
- **Octopuses have three hearts and blue blood.** 🐙
- **Honey never spoils**—you could eat 3000-year-old honey! 🍯
- **There’s a species of jellyfish that is biologically immortal.** 🪼
